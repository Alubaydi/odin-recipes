Shish Barak

Description

Shish Barak is meat filled dumplings and cooked with yogurt soap

Ingredients

  • Ground meat beef or lamp
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Seven Spices Baharat
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Get Dough Prepared
  • Yogurt and water mixed
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt for the Yogurt sauce

Steps

  1. First, prepare the Dough
  2. Cook the Ground meat mixed with all the Ingredients
  3. Roll and cut the Dough
  4. Than, fill and shape the dumplings
  5. Prepare the yogurt sauce
  6. Begin, to cook the meat dumplings in the yogurt sauce
  7. Stir on low heat for 15-20 minutes
  8. Lastly, should be finished and ready to serve
