Hamburger

Description

This is Hamburger american cheese with with veggies

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onions
  • Lettuse
  • Pickles
  • Tomato
  • American Cheese
  • Mayoonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Mix the ground beef in portions
  2. Season the ground beef with Paprika and Garlic Powder
  3. Press indetaion in the center will help the burger cook
  4. Get fry pan ready and add oil to pan
  5. Heat the pan to medium level
  6. Add the ground beef burger once the pan is heated
  7. Let the burger fry for 15-20 minutes
  8. Make sure to flip burger each time to cook on both sides well
  9. Get buns ready before the burger is finished
  10. Add american cheese to the bun and heat it in the oven
  11. Once the burger is cooked and bun is heated add lettuce
  12. Add mayo on top of the lettuce
  13. Add burger on top
  14. Add Tomato and Onions than pickles on top
  15. Add the mustard on top of the toppings
  16. Lastly add the top bun above the burger
