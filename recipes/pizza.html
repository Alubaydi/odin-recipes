Pizza

Description

Pizza is a world famous dish made with dough, suace, cheese, and toppings of your choosing.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Suace
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Different types of toppings such as: Meat, chicken, Vegetables, and more

Steps

  1. Preparing and mixing Ingredients to make the dough
  2. Prepare the dough and roll it to make either a circle or square shape
  3. Get the sauce mixed around the dough roll
  4. Fill with Mozzarella cheese
  5. Once the cheese on top you can chose any toppings of your choosing
